# UNTRAP

> **Helping students escape confusion and build a career path aligned with who they truly are.**

Many students enter engineering without deeply understanding whether it aligns with their interests, strengths, or long-term goals.

They chose it because everyone else did — or because their parents expected it — or because they didn't know what else to do. Now they're two years in, building someone else's future, stuck inside what I call the **invisible trap**: family pressure, financial anxiety, societal conditioning, no structured path out.

UNTRAP is built for them.

It's not a career quiz. It's not a personality test. It's a personal operating system for a student's future — one that starts with understanding who you actually are, then builds a roadmap from there.

---

## Why UNTRAP Exists

The traditional career system answers one question:

> *"What career should you choose?"*

But students need answers to deeper ones:

- Who am I?
- What am I naturally good at?
- What path fits my actual reality?
- How do I get there, step by step?
- How do I handle pressure from the people around me?

UNTRAP exists to answer those questions.

---

## How UNTRAP works?

### 1. Identity Setup
Student creates a profile — age, education level, language preference. UNTRAP supports English and Hindi, with regional languages planned.

### 2. Deep Self-Discovery Assessment
Not the usual "pick your hobby" quiz. UNTRAP asks what makes you lose track of time, what people come to you for, how you think about risk, money, freedom, impact. It maps your interests, strengths, personality, and real-life constraints — all together.

### 3. Trap Identification
The AI helps students identify possible barriers holding them back.

- **Career Trap** — chose a path because everyone else did
- **Family Trap** — facing pressure or lack of support at home
- **Awareness Trap** — doesn't know what options exist
- **Skill Trap** — knows the direction, doesn't know how to start
- **Confidence Trap** — ability is there, belief isn't

### 4. Personal Career Map
UNTRAP generates *Your Career Universe* — three to five paths that genuinely match the student's profile, each with why it fits them, skills required, income potential, difficulty level, and the first concrete steps.

### 5. Personalized Roadmap
From 5-year vision down to today's actions.

```
5-Year Goal → Year Plan → Quarterly Goals → Monthly Goals → Weekly Goals → Today
```

Every level is generated around the student's specific situation — not a template.

### 6. AI Mentor — Saarthi
An AI companion that remembers the student's goals, personality, and progress. They can ask it anything:

> *"Should I leave engineering?"*  
> *"My parents don't support my choice. What do I do?"*  
> *"What should I actually work on today?"*

Saarthi gives contextual advice — not generic motivation.

### 7. Progress Tracking
Daily reflections, weekly reviews, monthly growth reports. UNTRAP tracks career growth, skill development, goal progress, and personal clarity — not just completion percentages.

### 8. Family Alignment Layer *(optional)*
Students can share a structured breakdown of their chosen path with their parents — career data, income possibilities, roadmap — to reduce conflict and open the conversation.

---

## Architecture

UNTRAP is built around four intelligence layers:

**1. Self-Discovery Engine**  
Understands the student's interests, strengths, values, and real-world constraints through structured assessment.

**2. Career Intelligence Engine**  
Maps possible career paths that match the student's profile — with reasoning, not just labels.

**3. Action Planning Engine**  
Converts long-term goals into a practical, time-bound roadmap the student can actually follow.

**4. AI Mentor Layer (Saarthi)**  
Provides continuous, personalized guidance across the student's entire journey — context-aware and memory-backed.
